Solutions to homework 3 on regular expression puzzles.

Question 1:

Find: \s{2,}
Replace: ,

Explanation: Find all whitespace that consists of 2 or more whitespace characters

Question 2:

Find: (\w+), (\w+), (.+)
Replace: \2 \1 (\3)

Explanation: Capture first name, last name, and remaining words about university and then rearrange them

Question 3:

Find: .mp3\s
Replace: .mp3\n

Explanation: Find all instances of .mp3 then a space and replace the space with a newline

Question 4:

Find: (\w+) ([^.]+).mp3
Replace: \2_\1.mp3

Explanation: Get the number and then everything up to the period before .mp3. Song title_number with a .mp3 at the end.

Question 5:

Find: (\w).*,(\w+),(\w+.\w+),(\w+)
Replace: \1_\2,\4

Explanation: Capture first letter of genus, species, and numeric variables. Replace with first capture_second capture,last capture

Question 6:

Find: (\w).*,(\w{4}).*,(\w+.\w+),(\w+)
Replace \1_\2,\4

Explanation: Same as before but only capture first 4 letters of genus.

Question 7:

Find: (\w{3}).*,(\w{3}).*,(\w+.\w+),(\w+)
Replace: \1\2, \4, \3

Explanation: Same as before but only capture first 3 letters of genus and species. Replace in the required format/order.

Question 8:

Find: (\w+,\w+),\w+(.+0\n)
Replace: \1,0\2

Explanation: Highlighting all rows where pathogen_load is 0. Replacing the pathogen_binary value for such rows with 0.

Find: NA
Replace: 1

Explanation: After putting 0s for the rows where pathogen_load is 0, the remaining NA pathogen_binary entries should be 1.

Find: [\!\@\#\$\%\^\&\*\(\)\-\+\_\=]
Replace:

Explanation: Looking for any special characters (mainly alternatives to 1-0 on keyboard)

Find: (.*),(worker|male)\s*,(.*)
Replace: \1,\2,\3

Explanation: Looking for worker or male (in bee caste column) followed by zero or more whitespace. Drop the whitespace and retain the caste and then everything before and after the caste.
